About Me

Throughout my career, I have been fortunate to work for one the world's top beauty company, Sephora. I have had many opportunities to expand my artistry skills, coordinate events to share my knowlege of makeup and skincare application, and network with some of the most talented and knowledgeable makeup artists in the nation.

Recently, I decided to dive into the field of coding to bring my creativity to life. I was accepted into the University of Texas-Austin Coding Bootcamp program for web development. I hope to use the skills obtained in this program to further my career in a technical role.

I am very driven to progress as a developer, and enjoy helping people solve problems when I can.
