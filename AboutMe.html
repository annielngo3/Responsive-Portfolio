About Me

I've been passionate about art from an early age; my parents received multiple calls from the school principal, because their 3rd grade daughter sold her art for a $1per page...now that's a good business woman! Throughout my 10+ years of experience in art, I have channeled this passion into web design and development.

I attended Maryville University of St. Louis and obtained a Bachelor's of Science degree in Biochemistry to be in the medical industry. Recently, I wanted to pursue my passion in art and attended the University of Texas - Austin to learn Full Stack Web Development. I enjoy transforming ideas into beautiful and intuitive designs. It's all about functionality, experience and pleasing the viewer's eye.

When I'm not coding, designing or pushing pixels, you'll find me scoping out new restaurants to try, traveling to any destination where the temperature is above 60 degrees Farenheight, or training for powerlifting competitions. This girl can squat over 300lbs!
